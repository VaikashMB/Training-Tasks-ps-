Puttu is a steamed rice cake made from a mixture of rice flour and grated coconut, while kadala curry is a spicy black chickpea curry made with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of spices.Enjoy with your friends and family.rating is 4 out of 5.The combination offers a delightful contrast in textures and flavors, with the light and airy puttu complementing the robust and savory kadala curry.

MASALA DOSA

Masala Dosa, also called Masale dosey ( ಮಸಾಲೆ ದೋಸೆ ), is a South Indian dish. It is a type of dosa and has its origin in the town of Udupi in Karnataka. It is served with potato curry, chutneys, and sambar.Masala dosa is enjoyed not only in South India but has gained popularity worldwide for its delicious combination of textures and aromatic spices.

CHICKEN BIRIYANI

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish that includes layers of chicken, rice, and aromatics that are steamed together. The bottom layer of rice absorbs all the chicken juices as it cooks, giving it a tender texture and rich flavor, while the top layer of rice turns out white and fluffy.

KERALA MEALS

One of the traditional Kerala dishes is vegetarian and is called the Kerala sadya. A full-course sadya, consists of rice with about 20 different accompaniments and desserts, and is the ceremonial meal of Kerala eaten usually on celebratory occasions including weddings, Onam and Vishu. It is served on a plantain leaf
